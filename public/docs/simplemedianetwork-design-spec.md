## 7. IMAGERY RULES (ENFORCEMENT)

### Allowed

* Architectural detail
* Material texture
* Abstract systems
* Editorial portraits (avatars only)

### Forbidden

* Smiling stock people
* Literal “team” imagery
* Startup metaphors
* AI illustration styles
* Visual metaphors that explain content

Images frame seriousness — they do not teach.
